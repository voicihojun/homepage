AI Launch Lab

Training program in artificial intelligence

Montreal, QC, Canada

This programme was funded by the Quebec Ministry of Economy and Innovation. This training is specifically designed to provide undergraduated students in Quebec with work experience, mentoring, theoretical and practical courses, and a professional network.

Through a number of workshops within this program, it was an opportunity to increase the overall understanding of artificial intelligence. Among them, NER, labeling, classification, data preprocessing, and analysis were performed while using languages and libraries such as python, spacy, and prodigy for the actual project for NLP.
